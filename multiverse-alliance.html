Arcana, Multiverses Relectant Savior

Arcana, Multiverse’s Reluctant Savior, is a variant of Darkness who conquered her chaotic universe through manipulation and precision, forging a flawless utopia free of war, poverty, and unpredictability. Yet in her triumph, she discovered the cost of perfection—a lifeless, sterile world that left her bored and restless. To stave off her emptiness, she orchestrated small conflicts, creating drama within her controlled dominion. When Persephone, a force of pure chaos, threatened to unravel the multiverse, Arcana reluctantly stepped into the role of savior. Fighting not out of heroism but necessity, she used her unmatched power to manipulate and repurpose others’ resources, turning chaos into order. As she battles Persephone, Arcana faces an internal struggle: her disdain for chaos is matched only by her craving for the vibrancy it brings.
