Darkess, The Apostate

Darkness, the enigmatic patriarch of the Shadows, is a relentless force of despair and ambition. A master tactician and manipulator, he wields the power to shatter morale by twisting the things people love most into terrifying forms. Leading a sect of power-armored warriors, he envisions a future forged through destruction, where only the strongest and most resilient survive. Driven by a deep desire to be needed and valued, his complex relationship with Theresa, his former ally and greatest adversary, underscores the tragedy of his fall. Zora, The Ghost

Zora, one of the seven original Shadows, is a master of stealth and precision who turns silence into her deadliest weapon. As a shapeshifter, she takes on the forms of long-forgotten individuals, wielding both her physical prowess and psychological cunning to sow fear and confusion. Leading her sect, the Silent Knife, Zora specializes in gathering intelligence and executing surgical strikes with chilling efficiency. The Spirit, Master of Death

The Spirit, the master of death arcana among the seven original Shadows, wields unparalleled knowledge of life and the beyond.
